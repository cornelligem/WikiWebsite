OVERVIEW
At the start of our project, we had found a novel technical solution which numerous field applications. As a result of our conversations, inspiration and extensive research, we chose to focus our project around the hydroponics due to its great potential for advancement. Throughout the design and building processes of our project, we sought out and interacted with a variety of experts in the field ranging from hydroponic farmers to supply distributors and even to professors considered pioneers in the world of hydroponics and plant science. The expertise and personal accounts these individuals shared with us were critical to our projectâ€™s development, from the beginning stages of conceptualization to the end creation of our product. Their insight and feedback was the backbone for every choice that we made in every stage of the project, since we knew that the users themselves would know best what would work and be successful within the industry and with the scientific community.

Looking to the future of our biocircuit, considering all the possible applications, we talked to researchers in a variety of fields that deal with oxidative stress to better understand the next steps for our project. Furthermore, we sought to understand other aspects.
